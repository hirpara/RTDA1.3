LOOK IN THE MIRROR. THAT’S YOUR COMPETITION

If you like dance, raise your hand. If not, raise your standards. RT Dance Academy is the epitome of phenomenal and will make you as good as a professional! With an aim to provide quality dance training and making dance available to all, RT Dance Academy runs throughout the year training in various dance styles. The classes give you an opportunity to learn dance, express yourself creatively and perform with confidence on stage.


Rishab Totlani is the founder, who joins the artistic dots and creates dance from stories, emotions and ideas. RT Dance Academy believes in opening original ideas to new audiences and craft unexpected matches between classical & contemporary art forms. The bases of our work is on expressive integrity and technical quality, reconciling discord with grace. It’s usually not easy to find a teacher who is patient with beginners, but our dance academy is definitely one of them! Our children have learned and enjoyed themselves so much, and love sharing their new skills with us. We love to combine artistic vision with practical prowess to achieve the dance academy’s objectives by believing in the motto - 'Dance can transcend and break through many barriers that other things cannot.'
